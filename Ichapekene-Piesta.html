Ichapekene Piesta Inasianuana

For the last three centuries, in the remote town of San Ignacio de Moxos (located in Beni, Bolivia), the Moxos's indigenous council gather to organize the celebration of their famous Ichapekene Piesta Inasianuana. An event that besides being a fascinating spectacle to witness is a celebration of the resilient and fighting-spirit of the people of Moxos, because till today they have to fight; as they have done in the past, for their rightful claim of sovereignty over their lands and their autonomy.
